Explanations

What is revising?

Revising is evaluating a text's organization and its logical flow of ideas, and suggesting changes for improving those things. Revising involves larger text units (chapters, sections, paragraphs) and moving text around if required.

What is editing?

Editing is evaluating smaller text units (paragraphs, sentences, words) and fixing them. Editing-type changes include making sure that a paragraph's verb tenses agree and that idioms are used appropriately.
