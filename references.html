Jennifer C. Ast, PhD

Clear Text offers text editing and writing coaching for academic texts.

Clients

I have been writing and editing scientific texts for more than 20 years as part of my training as a professional biologist (my first peer-reviewed paper was my undergraduate honor’s project).

Recently I’ve been studying language and writing, and have been helping university students at all levels improve their written communications. I have worked on diverse texts, incuding primary scientific papers, popular summaries, and doctoral theses.

My most recent clients include doctoral students at the Department of Business Studies, and post-docs at the Departments of Molecular Evolution and Paleontology.
